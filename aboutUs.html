Despite numerous job portal website 'finding an appropriate job' remains an unsolved probrem. We thought of tackling this problem in our own way. We have created an interactive dashboard where only the essential information is displayed and further action is left to individuals. This way, interested job seekers and interested job posters can directly interact in a simpler way.

This website gives a common platform for job seekers, recruiters, referrers, job agencies etc for interacting and exchanging contacts. We wish to keep making this tool better based on your feedback and suggestions.
